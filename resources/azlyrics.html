    Writer(s): Caroline Ailin, Dua Lipa, Ian Kirkpatrick, Emily Warren
    "Don't Start Now" is the lead single off of her second studio album. The track was announced by Dua Lipa on Twitter on the 24th of October 2019: she posted a video snippet of herself with the song playing and the message "DON'T START NOW COMING SOON".
    Dua Lipa explaned to Billboard, "I chose to put this song out first so I could close one chapter of my life and start another. Into a new era with a new sound! It's about moving on and not allowing anyone to get in the way of that. It also felt like a natural first-song choice as I made it with the brilliant same crew I made 'New Rules' with."
    A trailer of the music video for this song was published on the 31st of October 2019, just hours before the release of the official video.
